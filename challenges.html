Challenge 1: Student Selection

You have been given a list consisting of dictionaries. Each dictionary has the name of the student, their age, and the marks obtained in the test. To be successful in the test there are two conditions:

  • The age of the student should be greater than or equal to 20
  • The average score should be greater than or equal to 80

Based on these conditions, create a list of students who have passed and print it.

students = [
{"name": "Alice", "age": 22, "grades": [88, 92, 79]},
{"name": "Bob", "age": 19, "grades": [75, 85, 80]},
{"name": "Charlie", "age": 23, "grades": [90, 87, 93]},
{"name": "David", "age": 21, "grades": [65, 70, 72]},
{"name": "Eve", "age": 20, "grades": [95, 98, 100]}
]
